Bruin Sports Analytics is a student-run sports statistics club that serves as a platform for sports research, data journalism, and machine learning applications.

In Spring of 2019, BSA became the official consulting partners of USA Hockey!

We are composed of a Data Journalism Team, which aims to produce monthly short entertaining articles, a Research Team, which works closely with our club advisor to publish at least 1 sports research paper a year, and a Consulting Team which provides data insight for our affiliates.
