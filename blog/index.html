The 3pt Revolution in the NBA

Nikhil Sharma
A visualization of how the number of three point attempts taken per game by team has drastically increased since the introduction of the three point line in the 1979-80 NBA season. Note that the team labels are updated as of the 2017-18 season; thus, teams that have changed names over the years correspond to the labels of the current name of the team. For example, the Vancouver Grizzlies are represented by the Memphis Grizzlies, the Charlotte Bobcats are represented by the Charlotte Hornets, etc.
